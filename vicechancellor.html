Dr. K. Narayanasamy

Vice-Chancellor, The Tamilnadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University

Biography

Born on 11th June 1963, Dr. K. Narayanasamy hails from an agricultural family in the village of Reddiapatty, located in Aruppukottai Taluk, Virudhunagar district in the State of Tamil Nadu, India. He pursued his early education in the same village.

With a notable career spanning over a period of 33 years, Dr. Narayanasamy has made significant contributions to medical services and education, particularly excelling in the specialized field of **Hepatology**.

Academic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Chemistry from Madurai Kamaraj University
  • MBBS from Madurai Medical College
  • MD in Internal Medicine from Madras Medical College
  • DM in Gastroenterology from Madras Medical College
